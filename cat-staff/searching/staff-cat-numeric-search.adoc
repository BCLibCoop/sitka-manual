Numeric Search
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The _Numeric Search_ pane allows you to search numerical fields in bibliographic records. You may search the following fields: _ISBN_, _UPC_, _ISSN_, _LCCN_, _TCN_, and _Item Barcode_.

NOTE: A record may have multiple barcodes on it. _Item Barcode_ refers to the barcode created by your library. It is the barcode you scan to check an item in or out.

First, select the _Query Type_ from the drop down menu. Then, enter the numeric value of the search in the _Value_ text box. 

Begin the search by clicking the *Search* button in the top-right corner. Search results appear below the search form.
