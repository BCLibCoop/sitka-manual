MARC Search
~~~~~~~~~~~

The _MARC Search_ pane allows you to search fields and subfields in MARC records.

. Go to *Cataloguing* -> *Search the Catalogue*.
+
image::images/cat/cat-menu.png[Catalogue Menu]
+
. Select the *MARC Search* tab. It will be open by default if it's set as your default search pane. 
+
image::images/staffcat/staff-cat-marc-search-1.png[]
+
. When searching it's important to ensure that your desired search scope is set in the library selector.
+
[TIP]
======
Multi-branch libraries can search their entire system or a specific branch.

Libraries participating in reciprocal borrowing may wish to search their library or their ILC zone.

You can set your default search library for the workstation in xref:_search_preferences[Search Preferences].
======
+
image::images/staffcat/staff-cat-library-selector.png[Library selector in the staff catalogue]
+
.. (MPL is the Maple Library on Sitka's training server.)
+
. Enter the tag, subfield, and value and click *Search*.
+
image::images/staffcat/staff-cat-marc-search-2.png[]
.. You can only search one tag or subfield per row. To add additional rows, click the *plus sign* (+) to the right of the search row. To remove rows, click the *minus sign* (-) to the right of the search row.
+
[TIP]
=====
Remove any apostrophes from your search value. No results will be returned when apostrophes are included.
=====
+
. Your results are displayed.  Refer to xref:_search_results[]
for additional information.
+
image::images/staffcat/staff-cat-marc-search-3.png[]
