MARC Search
~~~~~~~~~~~

The MARC Search pane allows you to search fields and subfields in MARC records.

Enter the three-digit tag you wish to search in the _Tag_ text box. If desired, enter the subfield you wish to search in the _Subfield_ text box. 

Then, enter the value for which you are searching in the _Value_ text box. Begin the search by clicking the *Search* button in the top-right corner. Search results appear below the search form.

You can only search one tag or subfield per row. To add additional rows, click the *plus sign* (+) to the right of the search row. To remove rows, click the *minus sign* (-) to the right of the search row.
