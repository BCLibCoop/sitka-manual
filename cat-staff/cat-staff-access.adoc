Accessing the Staff Catalogue
-----------------------------

There are three ways to access the _Staff Catalogue_.

[TIP]
=====
The _Staff Catalogue_ may also be accessed by pressing the *F3* key any time while using Evergreen.
=====

From the Splash Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

. On the splash page, enter a keyword into the text box in the _Item Search and Cataloguing_ section.
. Click *Search Catalogue*.
. The _Staff Catalogue_ page opens with the results of the keyword search for your default search library.

image:images/staffcat/access-staff-catalogue-1.png[The Item Search and Cataloguing section of the splash page 
with the search bar highlighted.]

[TIP]
=====
You can change your default search library for the workstation in the xref:_search_preferences[Search Preferences].
=====

From the Search Menu
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

. Go to *Search* -> *Search the Catalog*.
. The _Staff Catalogue_ page opens.

image:images/staffcat/access-staff-catalogue-2.png[Search menu with Search the Catalog highlighted.]

From the Cataloguing Menu
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

. Go to *Cataloging* -> *Search the Catalog*.
. The _Staff Catalogue_ page opens.

image:images/staffcat/access-staff-catalogue-3.png[Cataloging menu with Search the Catalog highlighted.]
