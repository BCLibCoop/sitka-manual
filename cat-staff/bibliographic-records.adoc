Viewing Bibliographic Records
-----------------------------

Each title in the catalogue is represented by bibliographic record. This record contains detailed information 
about the title. There may be multiple records for a given title if the title has multiple editions, formats, 
or publishers in your catalogue. While the goal is to have one MARC record in the database for each discrete 
bibliographic title which all libraries add their holdings to, in practice this doesn't always happen so libraries
may see duplication of records when searching beyond their library or system.

The bibliographic record display can be accessed:

* through the staff catalogue by clicking on the hyperlinked title or cover image in a search result
* throughout the staff client by clicking on the hyperlinked title in any grid it appears in
