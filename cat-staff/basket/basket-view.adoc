Viewing the Basket
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To view a basket, click the basket icon in the search form, located to the left of the *Basket Actions* button.

You may also click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *View Basket* to open the basket.

image::basket/view_basket.png[Screenshot of the search form with the basket icon and the View Basket menu option highlighted, as described above.]

Records appear in the basket as they appear in the search results, although _Facets_ are unavailable in the basket.
