Batch Actions Through the Basket
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Place Hold
^^^^^^^^^^

To place a hold on every item in the basket, click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *Place Hold*. The _Place Hold_ form appears, and a hold can be placed as it would be otherwise (for a single item). Whether or not an individual hold is successful will be indicated next to the record title.

Print Title Details
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

To print the details of each record in the basket, click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *Print Title Details*. A printer-friendly version of the basket will appear.

Records appear in the same order as in the basket, and are numbered in ascending order.

Each bibliographic record on the list contains the record's bibliographic ID number, ISBN, title, author, publisher, publication date, and item type.

Email Title Details
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

To email a summary of the basket contents, click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *Email Title Details*. A plaintext summary of the basket will be emailed to the address associated with your Evergreen account.

The email will contain information about each record's bibliographic ID number, ISBN, title, author, publisher, publication date, and item type.

Add Basket to Bucket
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

To add the basket to a bucket, click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *Add Basket to Bucket*. The _Add # Items to Bucket_ window appears. 

If you want to add the basket to an existing bucket, select the bucket name from the _Name of existing bucket_ dropdown menu, then click *Add To Selected Bucket*. The items are added to the bucket, the _Add # Items to Bucket_ window closes, and confirmation message appears at the bottom of the screen.

If you want to add the basket to a new bucket, click the *New bucket* tab. Enter a name and a description into the appropriate text boxes. Click *Add To New Bucket*. The bucket is created, the items are added to the bucket, the _Add # Items to Bucket_ window closes, and a confirmation message appears at the bottom of the screen.

If you want to add the basket to a shared bucket, click the *Shared bucket* tab. Enter the ID of the shared bucket in the text box. Click *Add To Shared Bucket*. The items are added to the bucket, the _Add # Items to Bucket_ window closes, and confirmation message appears at the bottom of the screen.

Export Records
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

To export the records in the basket, click *Basket Actions* and from the dropdown click *Export Records*. The _MARC Batch Import/Export_ page opens.

The _Record Type_ may not be changed.

Select the desired _Record Format_ from the dropdown menu. You may choose MARC21, UNIMARC, MARC XML, or Evergreen Record Entry.

Select the desired _Record Encoding_ from the dropdown menu. You may choose UTF-8 or MARC8.

If you wish to include holdings in the records export, check the checkbox. 

Click *Export* to download the file.
