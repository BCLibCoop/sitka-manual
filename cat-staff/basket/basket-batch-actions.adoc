[[_staff_cat_batch_actions_through_the_basket]]
Batch Actions Through the Basket
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The *Basket Actions* menu, found by clicking on the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_, 
lets you view the contents of the basket, batch place holds, print or email the title details for all titles, 
add the contents of the basket to a bucket, export records, and clear the basket.

[[_staff_cat_view_basket]]
View Basket
^^^^^^^^^^^

The contents of the basket can be viewed in two ways:

. Click the basket icon in the search form, located to the left of the *Basket Actions* button.
. Click *Basket Actions* and from the drop down select *View Basket*.

[NOTE]
======
Records appear in the basket as they appear in the search results, although _Facets_ are unavailable in the basket.
======

[[_staff_cat_basket_place_holds]]
Place Hold
^^^^^^^^^^

You can place a title level hold on every item in the basket

. Click the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_.
. Select *Place Hold* from the drop down menu.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-place-hold-1.png[]
+
. Enter the patron's barcode if you have it or click *Search for Patron*.
.. You can also use the radio buttons to select the logged in staff account.
+
image:images/circ/holds/title-hold-2.png[scaledwidth="75%"]
+
. The selected patron's information will populate.  Edit notification methods and pickup 
location if necessary and click *Place Hold(s)*. 
.. Any method or contact number entered on this screen will override defaults in patron record 
and will be used for display and print purposes for this hold.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-place-hold-2.png[]
+
. The _Holds Status_ field will update to indicate whether or not the hold was sucessfully placed.
See xref:_hold_placement_status_messages[] for the possible messages.
You can now place another hold for this title or click *Return* to go back to the search 
results.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-place-hold-3.png[]

[[_staff_cat_basket_print_title_details]]
Print Title Details
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

. Click the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_.
. Select *Print Title Details* from the drop down menu.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-print-title-details-1.png[]
+
. A printer-friendly version of the basket will appear. Records appear in the same order as in the basket and are numbered.
. Print using your browser's functionality. 

[NOTE]
======
The information that displays for each listed bibliographic record may vary depending on the information in the record. 
Bibliographic ID number, ISBN, ISSN, UPC, title, author, publisher, publication date, and item type can be included.
======

[[_staff_cat_basket_email_title_details]]
Email Title Details
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
. Click the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_.
. Select *Email Title Details* from the drop down menu.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-email-title-details-1.png[]
+
. A plaintext version of the of the basket will be emailed to the address associated with the library staff account currently 
logged into the staff client. Records appear in the same order as in the basket and are numbered. 

[NOTE]
======
The information that displays for each listed bibliographic record may vary depending on the information in the record. 
Bibliographic ID number, ISBN, ISSN, UPC, title, author, publisher, publication date, and item type can be included.
======

[[_staff_cat_basket_esport_records]]
Export Records
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

. Click the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_.
. Select *Export Records* from the drop down menu.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-export-records-1.png[]
+
. The _Export_ tab of the _MARC Batch Import/Export_ interface opens.
. The _Record Type_ cannot be changed.
. Select the desired _Record Format_ from the drop down menu. You may choose MARC21, UNIMARC, MARC XML, or Evergreen Record Entry.
. Select the desired _Record Encoding_ from the dropdown menu. You may choose UTF-8 or MARC8.
. If you wish to include holdings in the records export, check the checkbox. 
. Click *Export* to download the file.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-export-records-2.png[]
+
. A file will download to your browser and can then be downloaded to your computer.

[[_staff_cat_basket_clear_basket]]
Clear Basket
^^^^^^^^^^^^

. Click the *Basket Actions* button in the bottom right of the _Search Form_.
. Select *Clear Basket* from the drop down menu.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-clear-1.png[]
+
. The contents of the basket are removed and the count beside the basket icon will be zero.

[NOTE]
======
The basket will also empty every time you log out of Evergreen.
======
