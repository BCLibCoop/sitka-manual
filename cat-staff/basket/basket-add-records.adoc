Adding Records to the Basket
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

[TIP]
=====
To add records directly to a bucket and skip the basket see the xref:_adding_records_to_a_record_bucket[].
=====

To add a record to the basket

. Find the record by conducting a search in the _Staff Catalog_.
. Check the check box that appears between the result number and the cover image of the desired result.
. The record is added to your basket and the count beside the basket icon will increase.

To add all records on the page to the basket

. Check the check box between the search form and the search results. This will select all results on the page.

To add a record from an additional page

. Navigate to the page where the record appears and check the checkbox of the desired record.

To add all search results

. Click the *Basket Actions* button to expand the options, and click *Add All Search Results*.
+
image::images/staffcat/basket/staff-cat-basket-add-search-results-1.png[]
 

[CAUTION]
=========
If your search returned many results, this action may take a long time.
=========
